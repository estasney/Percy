About Percy


Percy is a next-generation sourcing catalyst for Cisco's Talent Hub Organization.


What could Percy do?

Percy... This resume is my ideal candidate. Find me more like it!

Percy... Show me resumes of Product Managers involved in "Connected Car".

Percy... Take these 1000 resumes and remove any where the candidate works for a Partner.


Why Talent Hub Needs Percy

Talent Hub is comprised of exceptionally talented sourcers.

Unfortunately, the majority of their time is spent performing work that does not allow them to showcase their talents.

Percy seeks to remove much of this burden, so Talent Hub can do what it does best!

Proposed Solutions


SEARCH: By integrating our searches with Percy, Booleans will become deprecated. Searches can be conducted based on intent, rather than content.


MANAGE: Percy can categorize candidate profiles into multiple subsets. With this, each message we send is custom tailored to each candidate. We can stay on top of our candidates through smart reminders.


LEARN: By collecting feedback from our users, Percy will continue to improve its insights and recommendations.
