Percy... I don't know what "Kubernetes" means, but the job requires it.

The concept of "word embeddings" is a powerful algorithm for quantifying the relatedness of words.

During its training Percy looked for where terms where used in similar contexts. So while Percy cannot tell you the definition of "Kubernetes" it can tell you other words that were used in similar contexts.

This feature can provide additional suggestions to "Kubernetes" for your search.
