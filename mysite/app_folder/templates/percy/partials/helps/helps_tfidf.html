This tool is incredibly useful for situations when you've found a great résumé and want to find more like it.


If you build out a search string from an ideal résumé it is easy to be too specific and you risk missing your next ideal résumé.


"Resume Fingerprinting" computes the probability of each word in your resume and compares it to a large body of résumés. Those with the greatest difference in probability are ranked highest


And that means...?

  • Words like "purple", "LTE", "numpy" score high because they occur infrequently
  • Words like "experience", "develop", "manage" score low because they occur frequently
