This tool is incredibly useful for situations when you've found a great résumé and want to find more like it.


If you build out a search string from an ideal résumé it is easy to be too specific and you risk missing your next ideal résumé.


"Resume Fingerprinting" uses a simple but highly effective NLP technique known as Term - Frequency, Inverse Document Frequency


When you enter an example résumé, Percy runs a tally of the number of times a word appears in your document. Each of these tallies is then divided by the total number of words. (To accommodate documents of any length). Then Percy makes use of its résumé training data to calculate "Inverse Document Frequency"


And that means...?

  • The more frequent a word is used in a résumé, the higher its score.
  • The more frequent a word is used in all résumés, the lower its score.
